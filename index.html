What We Do!

Empowering Children Through Comprehensive Support

At Donhodzo Children’s Trust, we are dedicated to providing children with the resources they need to succeed. Our programs focus on education, healthcare, and emotional support, helping each child reach their full potential.

Building a Better Future for Zimbabwe’s Children

With a focus on mentorship, resource distribution, and community involvement, Donhodzo Children’s Trust works tirelessly to create a brighter future for Zimbabwe's children, ensuring they have the opportunity to thrive.

Nurturing Hope and Growth for Every Child

At Donhodzo Children’s Trust, we provide a safe environment where children receive the care, support, and education they deserve. Through our holistic programs, we aim to nurture their potential and foster a more hopeful future.

About Donhodzo Children's Trust

Donhodzo Children's Trust is dedicated to empowering the next generation by offering a range of services that focus on education, healthcare, and emotional well-being. Our programs aim to nurture children, providing them with the tools to thrive in a challenging world. Through mentorship, community support, and resource distribution, we aim to make a lasting impact on Zimbabwe’s youth.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a safe and nurturing environment for children, where they can grow, learn, and flourish. We are committed to helping each child realize their full potential, providing resources and support to enhance their lives.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every child has access to quality education, healthcare, and emotional support, regardless of their background. Through our programs, we strive to be a beacon of hope and change in Zimbabwe.
